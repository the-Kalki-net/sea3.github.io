About Our Company

SEA3 Agriceutical is a start-up in the Department of Marine and Coastal Studies, School of Energy Sciences, Madurai Kamaraj University (MKU) at the level of Pre-Seed and Seed Stage initiated with the seed funding of RUSA-MKU 2.0. The start-up produce and promotes 100% natural, water-soluble Seaweed liquid fertilizer (SLF).
